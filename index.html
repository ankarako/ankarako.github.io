Antonis Karakottas

Welcome to my personal page. I am a Computer Vision/Graphics PhD candidate and a member of MUG at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTH). I also work as a Research Associate at ITI's VCL3D lab, at CERTH. I received my Electrical and Computer Engineering Diploma from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Greece, with my diploma thesis being supervised by the one and only Dr. Leontios Hadjileontiades. I used to work as a sound engineer and producer for 5 years while I was studying.
