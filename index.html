Antonis Karakottas

I am a Computer Vision / Graphics PhD candidate at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTH) and I work as a Research Associate at ITI's VCL3D lab, at CERTH. I received my Electrical and Computer Engineering Diploma from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Greece, and my main research interests are 3D reconstruction from images, physically based rendering and path tracing.
