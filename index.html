About

I obtained my Engineering Diploma from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Greece, and I worked as a sound Engineer in parallel. Currently, I am a research associate and developer, working at CERTH-ITI's Visual Computing Lab (VCL). My research interests are 3D-Computer Vision, Machine-Deep Learning and 3D rendering. I am a 3D enthusiast with a passion about Ray Tracing and my deep desire is to use current cutting edge technology and create innovative solutions to achieve interactive photorealistic 3D renders with software developed with extensibility and modularity in mind.
